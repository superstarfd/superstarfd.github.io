About Superstar FD

Hi, I’m Siân. I work with professional services firms who aren’t yet ready to employ (or are looking for their perfect fit) a full-time finance director but who would benefit from the advice, support and direction that a finance director can provide.

While I work as an outsourced FD, I like clients to feel like I’m one of their team, a trusted adviser with a vested interest in their business. I take time to understand what drives the people and the businesses I work with and together we create the tailored financial and strategic goals that will take their business to the next level.

I provide calm clarity in a world that can be confusing. Making sense of figures and data, I will provide timely and understandable reports for management, set budgets which plan for the future and work with existing finance teams, providing them with support and direction.

About Me

I qualified with ACCA in 2007 and have worked in a variety of industries such as banking, investment management and law, for global companies, UK companies and not for profits. For the last 15 years I’ve worked in senior level financial management roles, often for fast growth SMEs.

Finance Function Refresh

This is where many clients start. They are often unsure what areas I can help with or even where to start with understanding the state of their current finance processes. A Finance Function Refresh looks into how the finance function of a business currently operates and then provides a roadmap of changes to implement which would be beneficial to operations going forward. This is designed to give leadership a tangible report that gives them greater understanding of what’s working well and what could be working better.

We start with a discussion to understand the business’s goals, ethos and anything else that might be relevant. I then look into your existing systems, processes, data and reporting to understand how the business’s financial operations and data looks. This will involve looking at key things such as debtor and cash management, data records/cleanliness, systems used and processes followed.

My findings are then consolidated into a report which is presented to management. This report gives a clear view on how things are currently operating and provides a roadmap of changes to make which could improve systems, processes and general finance operations for benefits including improved cash flow, profitability and clearer management reports. I can often help with many of these changes, but there will be no obligation to instruct me further at this point. Some of the results these reviews have seen include:

Improved cash flow through automation of invoice production, changes to customer payment options and redesigning credit control processes

Improved profitability through removing inefficient processes and consolidating finance systems

Improved financial control and decision making through redesigning monthly management reports so data is more understandable to leadership
